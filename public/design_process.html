Our Process

Needfinding

We conducted 6 different interviews with individuals on Zoom and riding the CalTrain to get their thoughts and experiences on public transportation.
Slides:  PDF,  PPT

Experience Prototypes and POVs

We conducted 3 more interviews and generated POVs for three of our interviewees to better understand their experiences and hone in on their stories. We then used basic experience prototypes test the needs we discovered.
Slides:  PDF,  PPT

Concept Video

After brainstorming experiences we wanted users to have, we shot a concept video to simulate one user's potential experience and gained benefit from using our app.

Low Fidelity (Lo-Fi) Prototype

We generated 4 tasks for our app to perform and prototyped the general interace of the app around them. We then interviewed 3 testers to see how they interacted with the app and how well they were able to perform the assigned tasks.
